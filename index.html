Why this list?


Early July, a couple friends and I clubbed together to buy gifts for a good friend's birthday. When we gave her the nice dress, the book and the fancy necklace it struck me. It was all wrapped in plastic and paper. We had gifted her things that completed a rich collection of made in China jewelry, clothes never to be worn and books never to be read.

There, I committed myself to only gift useful, low waste gifts that will positively impact my friends lives and the world we live in.

I've created this list to help all those who want to gift things that are meaningful but don't know where to look at. If this proves to have value to many of you, I'll start discussions with suppliers so I can manage the logistics myself and avoid the waste related to Amazon shipping.
